Where can I find the extension?

You can find the extension in the Chrome Store. We hope you like it!

I found a bug, can you fix it?

If you find a bug, you are more than welcome to notify us at [Your Email]. We will make sure to respond to you and fix the issues you are facing as quickly as possible.

Why does Cat Companion need permission to "access data on all websites"?

This permission is needed for adding a cat companion to the website. Cat Companion DOES NOT collect any of your data or your browsing activity on any websites. We are very fanatical about privacy ourselves and your privacy is extremely important to us as well. If you want the extension to only load when you specifically ask for it, you can right-click on the icon of the extension and select the "when you click on the extension" option.

How can I support the extension?

You are more than welcome to support us! You can do it in many different ways. If you want to, you can leave a good review on our extension page and share a link to the extension on social media.

Why can't I activate Cat Companion on a certain page?

Chrome Extensions don't work on specific pages such as the new tab page and the Chrome Extension store page. Another reason why you might not be able to activate Cat Companion on the page can be because the extension just updated in the background and the page runs the old version. If you get this message you can always reload the page - if the issue persists it is because Chrome Extensions are not allowed on the page you are looking at.

Where can I see the extension in action before installing?

We created a nice promo video you can watch on Youtube. You are very welcome to watch the video and leave a thumbs up if you like it.

How often do you update the extension?

We try to update the extension as often as we can. This is usually twice a month.
